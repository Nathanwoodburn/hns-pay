    Usage

    How to accept payments

    After you create a account and set your payout address, and link, you can create a link with parameters.

    https://pay.hns.au/p/yourlink?amount=10&data=My%20Payment%20Data&redirect=https://nathan.woodburn.au
    amount

    Amount to charge user. If this isn't included the user will be prompted to enter an amount.

    HNS Pay will send you a webhook even if the amount isn't the same as the inputted one
    data

    Add custom data that will be sent to you when the payment has been sent to you

    redirect

    Include a link on the payment page to your url.
